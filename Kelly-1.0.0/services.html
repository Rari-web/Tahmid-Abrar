CURRENT BAND MEMBERS

These are the Current Band Members of LINKIN PARK

Emily Armstrong

Lead vocalist known for her powerful voice and emotional performances.

Mike Shinoda

Co-founder, rhythm guitarist, keyboardist, vocalist, and producer, known for his rapping, singing, and songwriting.

Brad Delson

Drummer and co-founder, he provides the powerful, dynamic beats behind Linkin Park’s sound.

Dave "Phoenix" Farrell

Bass guitarist and backing vocalist. Brings depth and texture to the band’s music with his bass lines and harmonies.

Joe Hahn

DJ, sampler, and visual artist. Famous for his scratching, samples, and directing many of the band’s music videos.

Colin Brittain

Drummer and co-producer for Linkin Park since September 2024, known for refined and rhythmic drumming
